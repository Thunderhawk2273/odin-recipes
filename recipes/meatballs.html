Description

Homemade meatballs. This recipe assumes that you are making the red sauce recipe as well.

Contains Gluten and Milk

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of white bread
  • Milk
  • 16oz 85% Ground Beef
  • 19oz Italian Sausage
  • 2 egg yolks

Steps

  1. Cut crust off white bread. break into finger sized chunks and add to mixing bowl.
  2. Add 1/4 - 1/2 cup milk. Let sit for 5 minutes, or until bread is soaked
  3. Add Ground Beef, Italian Sausage, and egg yolk
  4. Mix well. Use hands and gloves if you desire.
  5. Use scoop to make 1"-2" balls. Roll in hand to compress it.
  6. Place balls into heated skillet with olive oil.
  7. Cook one side until medium brown. Flip meatball.
  8. Cook other side until brown.
  9. Place into Sauce pan. Continue cooking sauce until cooked through.
  10. Bon Appetite!
