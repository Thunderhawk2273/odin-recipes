Description

Homemade Red Sauce for Spaghetti, the easy way.

Ingredients

  • 2 Tablespoons Olive Oil
  • Chopped Garlic, Yellow Onion
  • 28oz Tomato Sauce
  • 28oz Chopped Tomatoes
  • 6oz Tomato Paste
  • 1/2 cup Italian Seasoning
  • Salt, Pepper

Steps

  1. Combine olive oil, garlic, and onion in large sauce pan lit on low-to-medium heat. 2 minutes stiring
  2. Once garlic is lightly browned, add tomato sauce, chopped tomatoes, and paste. Stir until mixed.
  3. Add Italian Seasoning. Mix until well blended. Wait 5 minutes.
  4. Add salt and pepper to taste. (Roughly 1 teaspoon of each to start.)
  5. Keep on low heat for roughly 30-45 minutes. Add meatballs as the second side finishes cooking.
  6. Once meatballs are done cooking, turn off heat. Bon Appetite!
