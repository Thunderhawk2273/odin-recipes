Lazy Spaghetti

File:Cooked spaghetti.jpg. (2024, April 13). Wikimedia Commons. Retrieved August 6, 2025, from https://commons.wikimedia.org/w/index.php?title=File:Cooked_spaghetti.jpg&oldid=868026144.

Description

How to cook boxed spaghetti from the store.

Ingredients

  • Boxed spaghetti, Gluten or Gluten-free (your choice)

Steps

  1. Put medium pot with water on stove. Set flame to high.
  2. Once the water is boiling, add spaghetti to pot.
  3. Slowly stir noodles until submerged
  4. To test if done, taste until you like it, or throw 1 noodle at wall. If it sticks, it's done.
  5. Bon Appetite!
